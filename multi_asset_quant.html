04Walk-forward backtest

Weights are recomputed every rebalance interval using only data available up to that point (no look-ahead), then held until the next rebalance. Compared against an equal-weight rebalanced portfolio and a buy-and-hold position in the first asset (Equity), both under the same cost assumption.

Educational, client-side model only — not investment advice or a recommendation to buy or sell any security.
Backtested / simulated results do not represent actual trading and do not account for slippage, liquidity, taxes, or data-vendor adjustments beyond the simple cost estimate above.
