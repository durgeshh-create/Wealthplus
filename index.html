Strategy Reference
Active Strategy ETF Momentum Dip Buyer
OBJECTIVE

Systematically accumulate oversold ETF positions at market dips and exit once a target profit is reached, deploying LIQUIDCASE (a liquid debt ETF) as a float rather than holding idle cash. Every rupee is always working: either in an ETF position or in LIQUIDCASE earning carry.

UNIVERSE

A curated basket of NSE-listed ETFs configured in active_etfs (currently: MON100, GOLDBEES, SILVERBEES, JUNIORBEES, MINDSPACE-RR, EMBASSY-RR, BANKBEES). No symbol may appear in both Active and Dip Accumulator lists — validated at settings save.

BUY ENTRY — THREE TRIGGERS (any one fires)
Trigger A — Deep Oversold
W%R(14) ≤ williams_r_threshold (default −75)
No price-drop confirmation required. A deeply oversold W%R alone is sufficient to fire a first-slot entry.
Trigger B — Moderate Oversold + Drop
W%R(14) ≤ −60 AND price fell ≥ min_price_drop_pct% from prior close
Catches the zone between −60 and the threshold where oversold alone isn't decisive; a confirmed price drop adds conviction.
Trigger C — Gap Filler
W%R(14) ≤ −75 AND price fell ≥ min_price_drop_pct%
Fills the gap when the threshold is set tighter (e.g. −80) and Triggers A/B both miss a symbol at W%R −78 with a small drop.
SLOT SYSTEM (position scaling)
Slot 1 (first entry)Any trigger fires, symbol not yet held, no prior buy today. Fires immediately or at scheduled time.
Slot 2 + (averaging down)Symbol already held AND live price ≥ min_price_drop_pct% below blended avg buy price AND any trigger still active. Same flat % required for every additional slot — not cumulative.
Max slotsslots_count buys per symbol per day (default 5). Counter resets at midnight; avg price persists across sessions.
Cooldown5-minute lockout after each confirmed buy. One execution attempt per symbol per session (prevents duplicate orders if confirmation lags).
SELL EXIT
TriggerLive price ≥ profit_target_pct% above blended avg buy price (default 3%).
QuantityFull position — all held units sold in one order.
Proceeds recycledETF sell proceeds → LIQUIDCASE BUY (top-bid LIMIT). Cash is never left idle.
Resetprofit_target_pct and test_quantity reset to their saved defaults after each successful sell.
ORDER MECHANICS
Buy sizingBudget = max_cash_per_transaction. ETF qty = floor(budget / LTP). If max_qty_per_trade is set, quantity is further capped.
FundingSpendable cash used first; LIQUIDCASE sold only for the shortfall (ceil division). Cash reserve of ₹cash_reserve is always protected.
Order typeLIMIT by default — BUY at top-bid, SELL at top-ask from market depth. Falls back to LTP if depth is unavailable.
Market hours gateBuy execution blocked outside 09:15–15:25 IST. AMO auto-retry fires if a regular order is rejected outside hours.
Cash guardZerodha margin polled up to 45 s after LIQUIDCASE sell before placing the ETF buy, ensuring proceeds are credited first.
RISK CONTROLS
Per-stock capmax_cash_per_stock: total deployed per symbol never exceeds this. Guards against over-concentration in a single ETF.
LIQUIDCASE floorIf LIQUIDCASE value < max_cash_per_transaction, no new buy is queued — ensures float is never depleted below one transaction size.
Dry-run modeAll orders logged but not placed. Simulates full execution logic including LIQUIDCASE swaps.
Auth watchdogAny HTTP 403 from Zerodha triggers an exit-2 signal to the launcher, which re-authenticates and resumes automatically.
INDICATOR — Williams %R (14-period daily)

W%R = −100 × (Highest High14 − Close) / (Highest High14 − Lowest Low14). Ranges from 0 (overbought) to −100 (oversold). Today's candle is formed live using intraday high/low/LTP so the signal updates in real time through the session. Historical period uses daily OHLCV; the live candle replaces the incomplete current bar.

Dip Accumulator (B&H) Long-Term Position Builder via Tiered Dip Buying
OBJECTIVE

Build a long-term accumulation position in broad-market index ETFs (e.g. MID150BEES) by buying in proportionally larger tranches the deeper the price falls, measured by Williams %R oversold depth. Partial profits are harvested periodically to recycle capital while maintaining the core holding.

UNIVERSE

Symbols listed in bnh_symbols (currently: MID150BEES). Each symbol runs its own independent state, tier-firing set, and trade log. Multiple symbols supported simultaneously, each with separate caps and slot tracking.

5-TIER EXPONENTIAL SIZING — buy bigger the deeper the dip
W%R ≤ −60
5% of max ETF cap
Tier 1 — Probe
W%R ≤ −70
10% of max ETF cap
Tier 2 — Build
W%R ≤ −80
20% of max ETF cap
Tier 3 — Add
W%R ≤ −90
30% of max ETF cap
Tier 4 — Load
W%R ≤ −95
35% of max ETF cap
Tier 5 — Max Load

Each tier fires at most once per dip cycle. Tiers re-arm when W%R recovers above −40 (dip cycle reset). Total allocation across all five tiers = 100% of bnh_max_cash_per_etf.

BUY ENTRY CONDITIONS
SignalDaily W%R(14) ≤ −60 (oversold threshold, hardcoded for BnH).
Subsequent buysLive price must be ≥ 0.5% below current avg buy price before adding. Prevents buying into a position that has recovered.
Execution timeChecks every 15 s. Fires at 3:15 PM IST by default, or anytime if buy_execution_time = "anytime".
Per-symbol capCumulative deployed per symbol capped at bnh_max_cash_per_etf (₹10L). Per-transaction capped at bnh_max_cash_per_transaction (₹30K).
One buy per daybuy_attempted_today flag prevents multiple entries in the same session. Resets at midnight.
PARTIAL PROFIT HARVEST
TriggerLive P&L ≥ bnh_partial_profit_pct% (currently 7%) above blended avg buy price.
Quantity50% of held position sold — core holding preserved for further accumulation.
Cooldown30-day lock after each harvest. Prevents churning the position in a volatile recovery.
Order typeLIMIT at top-ask from market depth; falls back to LTP if depth unavailable.
No full sellUnlike Active Strategy, BnH does not sell the full position at a fixed profit target. The position is held indefinitely and only partially trimmed.
EXECUTION & FUNDING
Funding sourceAvailable cash used first; LIQUIDCASE sold for any shortfall, same smart-buy flow as Active Strategy.
Order typeLIMIT at top-ask (BUY) / top-bid (SELL) from depth. Shared default_order_type setting.
ThreadRuns in a dedicated background thread (BnHEngine), independent of the main signal loop. Each symbol checked every 15 s.
KEY DIFFERENCES FROM ACTIVE STRATEGY
Hold durationIndefinite vs. Active's fixed profit-target exit.
Sell behaviourPartial harvest (50%) only vs. Active's full position exit.
Position sizingExponential tier fractions vs. Active's flat per-transaction budget.
Oversold thresholdFixed −60 (BnH) vs. configurable williams_r_threshold (Active).
Symbol exclusivityNo symbol may be in both lists — enforced at settings save.
