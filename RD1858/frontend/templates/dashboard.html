Active Strategy — Multi-ETF Williams %R Dip Buyer

📋 Overview

Monitors a basket of NSE ETFs in real-time. Uses Williams %R (14-period, daily bars) to identify oversold dips and buys in configurable tranches (slots). Exits each position individually once it reaches the profit target. Capital is always parked in LIQUIDCASE between trades.

🟢 Entry Conditions
Trigger A Williams %R (14) ≤ −80 — deep oversold, fires regardless of price drop
Trigger B Williams %R (14) ≤ −60 AND LTP has dropped ≥ Min Price Drop % from previous close
Slot 1 Fires when Trigger A or B is first met for the day
Slot 2–N Fires only if a prior buy exists (can be from a previous day) and live price has fallen ≥ Min Price Drop % below the current avg buy price. Same flat threshold applies to every additional slot — blended avg buy price is used, so this works correctly across multiple sessions
⏰ All buy orders execute at the configured Order Execution Time (default 3:15 PM). Use Force Execute to bypass.
🔴 Exit Conditions
Profit Hit P&L % for the ETF reaches ≥ Profit Target % (checked continuously during market hours)
Sells the full position in one LIMIT/MARKET order at top-ask. Settings auto-reset to Saved Defaults after each sell.

Profit targets reset to Saved Defaults after each sell. W%R re-evaluated on each new daily candle close.

Dip Accumulator & Harvester — Accumulate on Dips · Harvest at 5%

📋 Overview

A dual-phase CNC accumulation engine for ETFs in your BnH universe (see Manage Symbols). On every oversold dip, the system deploys capital across up to 5 conviction tiers — each tier fires exactly once per dip cycle based on Williams %R depth. At 3:15 PM, if the position is in profit the engine automatically harvests 50% and re-deploys into the next cycle. The core half-position compounds indefinitely.

🟢 Accumulation Phase — 5-Tier Buy Engine

Each tier corresponds to a W%R depth band and allocates a fixed fraction of Max Cash / ETF. Each tier fires at most once per dip cycle. A dip cycle ends when W%R recovers above −40, resetting all tiers for the next dip.

Tier 1 — Probe W%R ≤ −60 · deploys 5% of Max Cash / ETF · lightest conviction entry
Tier 2 — Mild Dip W%R ≤ −70 · deploys 10% of Max Cash / ETF
Tier 3 — Oversold W%R ≤ −80 · deploys 20% of Max Cash / ETF
Tier 4 — Deep Panic W%R ≤ −90 · deploys 30% of Max Cash / ETF
Tier 5 — Extreme W%R ≤ −95 · deploys 35% of Max Cash / ETF · maximum conviction buy
📌 Total max deployment across all 5 tiers = 100% of Max Cash / ETF (5+10+20+30+35). Each tier fires once and is locked until the dip cycle resets (W%R > −40).
⏰ All buy orders execute at the configured Execution Time (default 3:15 PM IST). Select ⚡ Anytime to execute as soon as the buy condition is met.
🔴 Harvest Phase — Partial Profit Exit
Harvest Trigger At execution time, if unrealised P&L ≥ 5% — automatically sells 50% of units held (MARKET or LIMIT). Cooldown: one harvest per 30 days.
Core Position The retained 50% never sells automatically — it compounds permanently. All tiers are available again for the next dip after the dip cycle resets.
💡 Harvested cash is parked back in LIQUIDCASE and re-deployed into the next dip cycle via the 5-tier system — a self-funding accumulation loop.
